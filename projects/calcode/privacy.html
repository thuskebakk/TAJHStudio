Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Calcode does not require an account. There is no sign-up, no login, and no user profile stored on any server.

Food logs, saved products, goals, reminders, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to Calcode or any third party.

Barcodes may be sent to Open Food Facts to fetch product information. No other data is included in that request.

In-app purchases, including Calcode Pro, are handled by Apple's StoreKit. Calcode does not process or store payment information.

Local notifications for reminders are scheduled on your device and never leave it.

What Calcode doesn't do

  • No analytics.
  • No tracking.
  • No ads.
  • No selling user data.

You can delete individual logs, individual products, or all app data at any time from Settings.
