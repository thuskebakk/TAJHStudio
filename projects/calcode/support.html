Support

Calcode is developed by TAJH Studio. For help with Calcode, contact support@tajhstudio.com.

Common questions

Restoring a purchase Restore purchases from the paywall or from Settings.
Managing or canceling a subscription Manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID subscription settings in the App Store.
Camera permission Camera access is required for barcode scanning.
Notification permission Notification access is required for reminders.
Missing or incorrect product data Barcode lookup uses Open Food Facts. Product data may be missing or inaccurate.
Deleting your data Delete individual logs or products, or delete all app data, from Settings in Calcode.

Refunds

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. TAJH Studio cannot issue App Store refunds directly.
